Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

We built BASED to help people find meaningful relationships grounded in shared values. Your personal data is used only to make that possible — nothing else. We do not sell your data, run advertising, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Who we are

BASED is operated by David Rhodes ("we", "us", "our"). If you have any questions about this policy or your data, you can contact us at the address at the bottom of this page.

2. What data we collect

When you create a profile, we collect:

  • First name
  • Date of birth
  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — we never see it in plain text)
  • Postcode or location (used to calculate distances between users)
  • Biological sex (used to show you opposite-sex matches)
  • A profile photo (uploaded by you)
  • Your answers to the 12 values questions (numerical scores on a scale of −10 to +10)

When you use the app, we also collect:

  • Actions you take on other profiles (Interested, Move on, Don't show again)
  • Messages you send to mutual matches
  • Whether your profile is set to hidden or visible
We do not collect your precise GPS location. Your postcode is converted to a latitude/longitude coordinate via a UK government-registered geocoding service (Postcodes.io) solely to calculate distances. Your exact postcode is never shown to other users.

3. How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Calculate compatibility scores between you and other users
  • Show you profiles of users within your chosen distance
  • Enable mutual matches to message each other
  • Enforce the free profile view limit and subscription status
  • Allow you to hide, unhide, or delete your profile

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the BASED service.

4. Who we share data with

We use the following third-party services to operate BASED:

  • Supabase — our database, authentication, file storage, and real-time messaging provider. Your profile data, photos, and messages are stored on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Postcodes.io — a free, open-source UK postcode lookup API used to convert postcodes to coordinates. No personal data is transmitted; only the postcode itself is sent.

We do not share your data with any other third parties. We do not sell your data. Ever.

5. Your profile and visibility

By default, your profile is visible to other users of the opposite biological sex within your chosen distance. You can:

  • Hide your profile — your profile will not appear in any searches. You can unhide it at any time from your profile settings.
  • Delete your account — this permanently removes your profile, photo, answers, interactions, and messages. This cannot be undone.
Your answers to the 12 questions are shown to other users on your compatibility screen, but they are presented as numerical scores — not as free text or direct statements attributed to you by name.

6. Messages

Messages are only possible between mutual matches — users who have both expressed interest in each other. Messages are stored in our database to enable conversation history. They are not read by us except where required to investigate a report of abuse or a legal obligation.

7. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

8. Security

Your password is never stored in plain text — it is hashed using industry-standard methods via Supabase Auth. Your profile photo is stored in a private Supabase storage bucket. All data in transit is encrypted using TLS.

While we take security seriously, no system is completely immune to risk. Please use a strong, unique password for your BASED account.

9. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

You can also delete your account and all associated data directly within the app at any time: My Profile → Delete my account.

10. Children

BASED is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a user under 18 has registered, please contact us and we will remove their account promptly.

11. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users via email or an in-app notice.
